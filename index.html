Welcome, Unkindled One

Journey into the ravaged lands of Lordran, where ancient lords and forgotten deities once held sway. The age of fire is fading, and the world is slowly succumbing to the encroaching darkness.

As an Unkindled, you are bound to the curse of the Darksign, destined to rise from your grave time and again, to link the fire or usher in an age of dark. Your path is fraught with peril, monstrous foes, and the echoes of fallen heroes.

Prepare to die, often. But remember, every death is a lesson, every failure a step closer to understanding the harsh, unforgiving beauty of this dying world.

Do you have the will to face the Abyss? To confront the Lords of Cinder and determine the fate of the dwindling flame?
